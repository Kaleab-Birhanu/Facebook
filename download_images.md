# Sample Images for Facebook App

## Free Image Sources:

### For Beach Post (beach.jpg):
- Search "beach sunset" on Unsplash.com
- Download and rename to `beach.jpg`

### For Cover Photo (cover_photo.jpg):
- Search "landscape" or "nature" on Pexels.com
- Download and rename to `cover_photo.jpg`

### For Marketplace Items (item1.jpg - item4.jpg):
- Search for: "phone", "laptop", "furniture", "car"
- Download 4 different product images
- Rename to `item1.jpg`, `item2.jpg`, etc.

### For Profile Friends (friend1.jpg - friend3.jpg):
- Search "portrait" or "person" on Pixabay.com
- Download 3 different people photos
- Rename to `friend1.jpg`, `friend2.jpg`, `friend3.jpg`

## Quick Download Links:
- Unsplash: https://unsplash.com
- Pexels: https://pexels.com
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com

## Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: Any size (Flutter will resize automatically)
- Recommended: 800x600 or larger for good quality
